    My home

    This is my home, my sweet sweet home.

    For you my Love,

    Forgive me,

    If I stumble and fall,

    for I know not how

    to love too well.

    I am clumsy,

    and my words

    do not form as I wish.

    and let my lips,

    point it for you,
